## 6. Content Requirements for Nana

### 6.1 Homepage Content

| Section | Content Required | SEO Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Hero headline | H1 with primary keyword + subheadline with USP | Captures primary keyword |
| Hero description | 2-3 sentences including "rent oxygen machine" and "buy oxygen machine" | Keyword density in first 100 words |
| Product cards (×4) | Name, 2-sentence description, price, CTA | Product-specific keywords |

### 6.2 Location Page Content

| Section | Content Required | SEO Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Hero headline | H1: "Oxygen Machine in {City}" + delivery subheadline | Geo-modified keyword in H1 |
| Location intro | 3-4 unique sentences mentioning city 2-3 times | Unique content; local signals |

| Nearby locations | Links to 3-5 nearby cities | Internal linking |

**Word count target:** 500–800 words per location page variant.

**CRITICAL:** No duplicate content across 127 pages. Vary by: geographic context, local landmarks, delivery framing, user scenario, nearby areas.

## 8. Keyword Cannibalization Prevention

| Query Type | Target Page | How to Enforce |
|---|---|---|
| "oxygen machine Malaysia" | Homepage | National keyword in H1/title; highest authority |
| "oxygen machine {city}" | Location page for that city | City in URL, title, H1, meta, body |
| "rent oxygen machine" (no city) | Homepage | Action + product without city modifier |
| "rent oxygen machine {city}" | Location page | Action + product + city |
| "Oxihome" (branded) | Homepage | Brand most prominent on homepage |

**Key rule:** Homepage NEVER uses a specific city in its H1/title. Location pages ALWAYS include their specific city.
