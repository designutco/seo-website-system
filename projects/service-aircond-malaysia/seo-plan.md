## 5. Content Requirements for Nana

### Homepage: 800-1200 words
1. Hero — H1 + value prop + WhatsApp CTA
2. Services Overview — 4 service types (servicing, installation, repair, chemical wash)
3. Why Choose Encik Beku — 4-6 trust signals
4. Location Grid — all 38 areas grouped by region
5. Social Proof — 3-5 testimonials
6. FAQ — 5 questions (all 3 languages)
7. Final CTA — WhatsApp button

### Location Pages: 600-900 words (unique per city)
1. Hero — H1 with city name
2. Local intro — 2-3 unique sentences mentioning landmarks
3. Services in {City} — 4 services with city name
4. Why Encik Beku in {City} — 3-4 bullets, at least 1 location-specific
5. Nearby Areas — links to 3-5 nearby cities
6. FAQ — 4 questions, at least 2 location-specific
7. CTA — WhatsApp with dynamic phone number

**City name must appear at least 5 times per page naturally.**
